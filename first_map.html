Best Restuarants in Satellite Beach

This map was created by Trey Ecker and displays markers of his favorite resturants to eat at in his hometown of Satellite Beach. The four restuarants vary in types of cuisine, as listed below.

  • Long Doggers = American
  • Doubles = Hoagies and Salads
  • Dakine Diegos = Burritos
  • Niki's Rainbow Diner = Greek-American
